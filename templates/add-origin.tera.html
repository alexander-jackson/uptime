Supported URL formats

✅ Valid examples:

  • https://example.com
  • https://www.example.com
  • https://api.example.com/health
  • http://localhost:3000

❌ Invalid examples:

  • example.com (missing protocol)
  • www.example.com (missing protocol)
  • ftp://example.com (unsupported protocol)
  • just-text (not a URL)
